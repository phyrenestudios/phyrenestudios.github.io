About

Phyrene Studios is a two-person game development team that creates expansive Minecraft mods introducing real-world concepts and processes.

Project Rankine

Available using Forge ModLoader for Minecraft Java Edition versions 1.16.5 and 1.18.2.

The mod focuses on implementing a wide variety of features based on materials science, chemistry, geology, and other fields. One of the foremost priorities of the mod is customization and creativity, allowing several different methods of progressing through the mod and a fully customizable config.
