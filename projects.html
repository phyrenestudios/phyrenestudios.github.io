Project Rankine

A customizable technology-based progression mod about material science, chemistry, and geology for Minecraft 1.16.5 and 1.18.2

Project Rankine takes inspiration from natural processes to bring over 50 stone types, hundreds of minerals and blocks, and the entire periodic table into the game. This mods tries to bridge the simplicity of vanilla mechanics with natural progression schemes with important chemistry interactions. This blend has led to a plethora of new and revised mechanics that leads players down new paths in the game.
