Project Rankine

A customizable technology-based progression mod about material science, chemistry, and geology for Minecraft 1.16.5 and 1.18.2

Project Rankine is an expansive mod that takes inspiration from natural processes to bring over 50 stone types, hundreds of minerals and blocks, and the entire periodic table into the game.

Download from Curseforge Download from Modrinth

World Generation

Through rock layering, new soil types, and new features, the world has a new look ripe for exploration compatible with many of your favorite biome mods.

Material Science

The periodic table becomes your playground as you explore different aspects of chemistry and material science. Research the properties of each element and create your own personalized alloy tools.

Expansive Systems

Progress through the mod in your own way, exploring various different mechanics such as treetapping, extracting resources from fluids, distilling atmospheric gases, and more.

Customizability

The features of Project Rankine are highly customiziable through the config to match how you want to play the mod. Custom recipes such as alloys, elements, and crushing can be added via datapack.
